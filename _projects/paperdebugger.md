[PaperDebugger](https://www.paperdebugger.com/) is an AI-powered academic writing assistant that lives inside your editor. It integrates directly into Overleaf and uses a plugin-based, MCP-orchestrated multi-agent system to help researchers write, review, and edit their papers — handling LaTeX syntax, formatting, and document structure so you can focus on ideas.

The work was accepted to the **WWW 2026** Demo Track {% cite hou2026paperdebugger %}.
